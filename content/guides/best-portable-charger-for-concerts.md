## FAQ

**Q: Can I bring a portable charger through stadium security?**
A: Generally yes — most venues don't prohibit them. Check the specific venue's bag policy before you go. The clear-bag requirement is more about what you carry it IN than what's inside.

**Q: What size portable charger is enough?**
A: 10,000 mAh covers two full charges for most phones. 20,000 mAh is more than a full show needs and adds weight. Unless you're running the crew's phones too, 10K is the sweet spot.

**Q: Is wireless charging worth it at a show?**
A: No. Wireless charging is 50-70% as efficient as wired, and you have to hold the phone still or prop it. Use a cable at a show. Wireless is for the nightstand.

**Q: What if the portable bank runs out too?**
A: That's what the 65W wall charger is for — top everything up at the hotel before doors. If you leave the hotel with a full phone and a full 10,000 mAh bank, you will not run out of battery at an Ella Langley show.
