## The venue: Soldier Field

Soldier Field sits on the lakefront at 1410 Special Olympics Dr. It is an open-air stadium, so weather is part of the plan -- June in Chicago can swing from hot afternoon sun to a cool lake breeze after dark. Dress in layers you can carry. The stadium also runs a clear-bag policy like most NFL venues, so sort that out before you leave the house.
