If you've got tickets to Morgan Wallen's Still The Problem Tour this year, Jason Scott & The High Heat might be the band that opens the gates -- and the difference between catching them and missing them is usually about an hour of showing up early. Here's the quick version.

## Who is Jason Scott & The High Heat?

Jason Scott & The High Heat are an Oklahoma City outfit playing full-band heartland country-rock -- tight, warm, and built for big rooms. They're the first act on several 2026 Still The Problem stadium bills, which means they play to the early crowd and win over whoever showed up on time.

## What time does Jason Scott & The High Heat go on?

As the opening act, they go on right at the listed show start -- which at these stadium shows has meant late-afternoon sets while the sun is still up. If your ticket says 4:30 PM, that is when this band starts playing, not when doors open.
