**Saturday, July 25, 2026** is the big room of the week — Ella Langley headlines North Charleston Coliseum, a 13,000-seat arena, and it's sold out. Here's the timing and the plan.

## Who plays when

Three-act headlining night:

- **Laci Kaye Booth** opens.
- **Kaitlin Butts** is direct support.
- **Ella Langley** headlines.

Doors open roughly an hour before the show. The first opener goes on at the listed start time on your ticket — that's the reliable number. Ella has been closing these nights after both openers, but we don't publish a guessed "she walks on at X" time. When we can't source the exact stage time, we tell you the listed start and leave it there.

In a sold-out 13,000-cap arena, the gate and the parking crush are the real variables. Give yourself 45–60 minutes before the listed start and you'll be in your seat, not in the concourse, when Laci Kaye Booth kicks it off.

## Before you go

North Charleston Coliseum is an indoor arena off Montague Ave with big adjacent lots — but a sold-out house fills them, so arrive early and screenshot your mobile ticket before you're in a dead-signal parking line. The room runs cool once it's full-and-air-conditioned; a light layer helps.
