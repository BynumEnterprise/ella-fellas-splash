## Outdoor-show tips for Koka Booth

Koka Booth is a lawn-plus-reserved amphitheatre tucked in Regency Park, so it's a proper summer-evening show. A late-July night in the Triangle can hold heat and humidity well past sunset — light clothes, and check the venue's clear-bag and lawn-chair rules before you leave.
