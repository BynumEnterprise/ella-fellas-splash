If you've got tickets to Morgan Wallen's Still The Problem Tour this year, Gavin Adcock might be the rowdiest slot on the Morgan Wallen bill -- and the difference between catching him and missing him is usually about an hour of showing up early. Here's the quick version.

## Who is Gavin Adcock?

Gavin Adcock is a Georgia-raised country-rock singer who played college football before music took over, and his live sets carry that linebacker energy -- raw vocals, heavy guitars, and a crowd that gets loud early. Songs like "A Cigarette" built his reputation on streaming, but the live show is the argument: he plays like the headline slot is his to steal.

## What time does Gavin Adcock go on?

On the 2026 Still The Problem Tour stadium shows, Adcock has played the mid-bill slot -- after the show start, before Ella Langley's direct-support set. At shows with a listed 4:30-5:30 PM start, that has put his set in the early-evening window, with the stadium still filling in. If you want to catch him, be in your seat within the first hour.
