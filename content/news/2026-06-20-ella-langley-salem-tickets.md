## Best seats at the Salem Civic Center

The Salem Civic Center is a reserved-seating arena that holds a few thousand, so there's no bad sightline, but a couple of things are worth knowing:

- **Floor, center** is where the energy lives. Ella works the front of the stage and the floor crowd sings every word back -- if you're here to be *in* it, this is the spot.
- **Lower bowl, side** is our value pick. You get an elevated view of the whole production, clean sound, and a seat you can actually stand in all night.
- **Up top** is the relaxed play in a room this size -- you'll still hear and see everything, often for noticeably less.
