## Why "direct support" matters

On a Wallen stadium bill there are usually three openers, and they go on in order of profile. The earliest act plays to a half-full stadium in daylight. The direct-support act plays last, to a full house, in the dark, with the production dialed all the way up. That is Ella's slot on these dates. It means a real set, a real light show, and a real crowd -- not a soundcheck with strangers.

It also means timing matters. Gates open hours before Wallen, and the openers run on a tight schedule. We always tell people: get in early, find your spot, and be standing when Ella's intro music hits.
