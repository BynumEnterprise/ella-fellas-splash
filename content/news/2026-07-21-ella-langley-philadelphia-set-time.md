**Saturday, August 1, 2026** — Ella Langley plays Lincoln Financial Field in Philadelphia on Morgan Wallen's Still The Problem Tour. It's a stadium night, nearly 70,000 people, and she's direct support — which means she's on *before* Wallen, earlier than a lot of casual ticket-holders expect. If you're going for Ella, this page is about not missing her.

## Who plays when

Four acts, and the listed start time is **5:30 PM** with doors listed at 4:30 — check your ticket, because those numbers matter more here than at any arena show:

- **Blake Whiten** opens the night.
- **Hudson Westbrook** follows.
- **Ella Langley** is direct support — she plays right before Wallen.
- **Morgan Wallen** closes.

The first act goes on at or near that 5:30 listed start. Nobody publishes per-artist stage times in advance and we won't invent one for Ella — but the math is simple: four acts, an early start, and she's third. On a bill like this her set lands while it's still light out, not at 9 PM. If you roll in on "headliner time," you will hear her from the concourse. Be inside by the listed start and you're safe.

One heads-up for anyone juggling tickets: the Linc hosts Wallen **two nights**, and Ella is only on the **Saturday, August 1** bill. Friday's show (July 31) has Brooks & Dunn in the support slot instead. Double-check which night you bought.

## Before you go

The Linc is an open-air NFL stadium in the South Philly sports complex — August in Philadelphia means it'll be hot when Blake Whiten starts and sticky when Wallen ends, so hydrate before you're through the gates. The stadium runs an NFL-style clear-bag policy; check the venue's bag rules before you pack anything. Parking lots around the complex fill early on a sold-out stadium night, and the Broad Street Line drops you at NRG Station right there — for a 70,000-person show, the subway is the honest play. Seating map and sightlines are on the [set-times and where-to-sit page](/tour/philadelphia-lincoln-financial-field-2026-08-01/set-times).

Sold out at face — the [Philadelphia show page](/tour/philadelphia-lincoln-financial-field-2026-08-01) points to verified resale. Always compare the all-in checkout total, not the sticker price.
