# Ella Langley at Red Rocks: How to Get Tickets for the Oct 7 Show

If you have been waiting for the right night to finally see her live, this is it. **Ella Langley Red Rocks tickets** go on sale this week, and the show -- Wednesday, October 7, 2026 at Red Rocks Amphitheatre in Morrison, Colorado -- is part of the new fall leg of the Dandelion Tour. Below is everything you need to actually land seats: the presale date, the general on-sale time, who is opening, and the smartest way to play a high-demand on-sale at a room that always sells fast.

## The Quick Answer

Ella Langley headlines Red Rocks on October 7, 2026, with Kameron Marlowe and Gabriella Rose opening. The artist presale begins Thursday, June 25, and the general on-sale follows Friday, June 26 at 10 a.m. MT through the official tour page. Red Rocks holds roughly 9,500 people and routinely sells out for country headliners, so the presale is your best shot, not the Friday morning rush.

Ella has said playing Red Rocks was a bucket-list item for her, and you can hear why it matters. After a year that put her single "Choosin' Texas" on top of the charts and turned the spring arena run into a string of sellouts, an open-air headline night under the rocks is the kind of full-circle moment fans will be talking about for years. Bucket-list shows draw bucket-list demand -- plan accordingly.

## Picking Your Seats at Red Rocks

Red Rocks is general-bench seating top to bottom -- there are no individual chairs, just numbered rows on the famous stone benches. Rows 1 through roughly 30 put you close to the stage, the middle rows trade a little distance for the best blend of sound and that postcard view, and the top is where the amphitheater works its magic on a clear Colorado night.
