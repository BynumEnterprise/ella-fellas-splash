## Best seats at the Live Oak Bank Pavilion

The Live Oak Bank Pavilion is an open-air amphitheater on the Cape Fear River that holds about 7,200, and the layout is simple: reserved seats up front, a general-admission lawn behind. Here's how we'd choose.

- **Reserved, center** puts you in the thick of it. Ella plays to the front of the stage and the close seats sing every word back -- if you want to be *in* the show, pay up here.
- **Reserved, side** is the value sweet spot: a clean, elevated angle on the whole stage, good sound, and a seat you can hold all night.
- **Lawn** is the easygoing play on a warm night -- bring a blanket, spread out, and you'll still hear every note rolling off the river. It's usually the cheapest way in, too.

Bags get checked at the gate, so a [clear stadium bag](/shop/ec-clear-stadium-bag) gets you through security without a second look.
