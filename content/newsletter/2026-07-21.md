# Evening Fellas.

It's Tuesday, July 21. No show tonight. The next one is Pikeville on Thursday. But Saturday in Baltimore gave us a story worth retelling. Here's the rundown.

## Top story

Picture the worst case. Song two of a sixty-minute stadium set, rain coming down sideways, 70,000 people at M&T Bank Stadium, and Ella's dress comes completely unzipped. Her fix took about ten seconds. She grabbed a purple Ravens jersey, pulled it on, picked the guitar back up, and finished the set. In Baltimore, the jersey read like a hometown move and the crowd ate it up. The next day she recapped it herself on her Story, soundtracked with maximum sarcasm by Stone Temple Pilots' "Wonderful": "Yesterday my entire dress came unzipped on song #2... in the pouring down rain in a stadium full of people." The set didn't lose a step either. Baltimore still got "Choosin' Texas," "Be Her," "you look like you love me," and the Morgan Wallen duet. A stadium-sized disaster, treated like a Tuesday. That's kind of the whole appeal.
