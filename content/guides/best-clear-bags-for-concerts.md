## FAQ

**Q: Do all concert venues require clear bags?**
A: Most major arenas and amphitheatres do, especially for stadium-format shows. Smaller clubs and theaters usually don't. Check the specific venue's website — it's always listed on the FAQ or ticketing page.

**Q: What size clear bag is the standard?**
A: 12"x6"x12" is the NFL standard that most venues follow. The crossbody in this guide meets that. When in doubt, smaller is safer.

**Q: Can I bring a small regular clutch as well?**
A: Most venues allow one small non-transparent clutch, 4.5"x6.5" or smaller, in addition to the clear bag. Your wallet, phone, and keys can go there. Check the specific policy.

**Q: What happens if I show up with the wrong bag?**
A: Security will ask you to return it to your car. There is usually no bag check at the gate. If you drove, you walk back. If you rideshared, you have a problem. Buy the right bag ahead of time.
