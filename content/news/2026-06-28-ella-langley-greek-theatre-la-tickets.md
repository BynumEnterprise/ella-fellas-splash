## Picking Your Seats at the Greek

The Greek Theatre is a fixed-seat amphitheater, so unlike Red Rocks you are choosing an actual section, not a bench row. The Terrace and lower rows put you close enough to read the setlist off the floor; the middle sections trade a few yards for a clean sightline and the best balance of sound; and even the upper seats stay surprisingly connected because the room is built into the hillside.
