## The Dandelion Tour just got a whole lot bigger

We've been watching the first leg of the Dandelion Tour sell out city by city, so this is the news a lot of us were quietly hoping for: Ella has added a second leg with 21 brand-new dates this fall. If you missed the first round, this is your second chance -- and there are a couple of stops on here that genuinely made us sit up.

## When and where the new leg runs

The fall run kicks off August 20 in Green Bay, Wisconsin, and carries all the way through October 31 in Saint Paul, Minnesota. In between, Ella's hitting markets across the country that the first leg skipped, so there's a real chance one of these lands closer to home for you than the spring dates did.

The two we keep coming back to: Red Rocks Amphitheatre in Colorado on October 7, and not one but two nights at Los Angeles' Greek Theatre on October 13 and 14. Red Rocks is a bucket-list room for any artist, and seeing Ella under those rocks after the year she's had feels like a full-circle moment. A two-night stand at the Greek says a lot about where her West Coast draw is right now too.

## Who's opening

Select dates on the second leg feature a stacked support lineup -- ERNEST, Kameron Marlowe, Laci Kaye Booth, and Gabriella Rose are all on the bill depending on the city. Openers vary by date, so check the specific show before you assume who you're catching. (If you've been following along, you'll recognize a few of these names from the first leg's all-women opener slate we wrote about.)

## How to get tickets

Here's the part to put in your calendar. The artist presale starts Thursday, June 25 -- you get access by signing up at Ella's official website ahead of time, so do that now rather than the morning of. General on-sale follows Friday, June 26 at 10am local time.

Given how fast the spring shows went, we'd treat the presale as the real shot here and the Friday on-sale as a backup. For the full routing and our city-by-city ticket guides, check our [tour hub](/tour), and if you're still deciding whether it's worth the trip, our recent [Oklahoma City show recap](/news/2026-06-19-ella-langley-oklahoma-city-recap-setlist) gives you a feel for what these nights actually deliver.
