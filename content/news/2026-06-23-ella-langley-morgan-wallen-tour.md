# Which Morgan Wallen Stadium Shows Does Ella Langley Play in 2026?

If you're trying to figure out where to catch Ella Langley on Morgan Wallen's tour, here's the short version: she's the direct support act on nine of the 21 stadium nights on Wallen's 2026 Still the Problem Tour, and the next one is this Saturday, June 27, in Clemson, South Carolina. She isn't on every date — Wallen rotates Thomas Rhett, HARDY, and Brooks & Dunn across the other nights — so checking the lineup before you buy matters.

Ella is in the middle of her own headlining Dandelion Tour at the same time, so these Wallen stadium stops are a separate, bigger-room way to see her. The two also released a duet, "I Can't Love You Anymore," and have shared a stage on this run more than once.

## How Is This Different From Her Own Tour?

On the Still the Problem Tour, Ella is the opener — expect a tight set of her biggest songs rather than the full Dandelion Tour show, and a stadium crowd that's there for Wallen too. On her own dates she plays a longer headline set with her full production. Both are worth it; they're just different nights out. If you want the deep-cuts-and-storytelling version, go to a Dandelion Tour stop. If you want the stadium-energy, two-headliners-for-the-price version, these Wallen dates deliver.
